Welcome

“Fortuna” means good luck

…and we hope you will be just as happy in our home in this fantastic destination as we are every time we go there. Chalet Fortuna is a haven of peace where happiness and relaxation await.

Set in a traditional wooden chalet, the apartment beautifully combines authentic Swiss charm with modern comfort. Renovated with great taste and love for detail, it comprises a living room, two double bedrooms, a third bedroom with bunk beds, a fully equipped kitchen with dining corner, and two bathrooms — one with a bathtub, one with a shower.

Step inside, unwind, and enjoy breathtaking views of the Matterhorn from the south-facing balconies.
