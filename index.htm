Welcome to Chalet Fortuna in Zermatt!

"Fortuna" is Latin for "good luck", so we hope that you will be just as happy in our home in this fantastic destination as we are every time we go there!

Description

Our holiday appartment is a sunny, cozy 3-bedroom appartment in the wooden chalet "Fortuna" and enjoys an amazing view of the Matterhorn (picture on the right).

The appartment has been renovated and refurbished with great taste and love for detail in 2010. The aim was to underline its wooden Chalet character while modernizing its amenities. It is composed of a living room, two double bed rooms, a third bed room with 2 beds (bunk-beds), a fully equipped kitchen with a dining corner and two bathrooms (one with a bathtub and another one with a shower).

You can find a a map of the appartment here.

    Access to the slopes

    The appartment is only a 5 minute walk away from the departure of the "Sunnega" mountain metro. To reach the ski area of Kleines Matterhorn, the easiest is to take the ski bus which is approximately 3 minutes by foot from the appartment. The Gornergrat Bahn is approximately 7 minutes by foot.

    You can find more information on the ski resort and the village on www.zermatt.ch
